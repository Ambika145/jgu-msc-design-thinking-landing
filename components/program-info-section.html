Programme Overview

M.Sc. in Design Thinking, Innovation & Strategy

Elevate your innovation and strategic capabilities with the online M.Sc. in Design Thinking, Innovation & Strategy, a comprehensive one-year online programme designed for emerging leaders and creative professionals. This prestigious Programme integrates advanced design thinking methodologies with strategic innovation frameworks to equip you with the tools to solve complex business challenges and drive transformative change across industries.

Through immersive learning modules, real-world case studies, and mentorship from global experts, you will develop critical skills in creative problem-solving, strategic decision-making, and innovation leadership. This programme is not just a degree—it is a catalyst for career advancement and a gateway to leadership roles in innovation-driven organizations worldwide.
